About Us

Stallon Gym: Fitness Redefined

Owned by Mr.Percy Surty and Mrs.Kashmira Surty, our Fitness center established in 1995 at Navsari and then in June 2008 Started branches in surat at Vesu, GhodDod Road, Althan, Adajan & Varacha, offering all types of fitness products & services under one roof. Our tagline "A GOOD COACH CAN CHANGE YOUR BODY, A GREAT COACH CAN CHANGE YOUR LIFE" reflects our commitment to a holistic approach towards fitness. We strive to be India's best health hub, providing exceptional and innovative programs and services that result in... measurable and transformational changes in the lives of our members. Join us on our journey towards a fitter and healthier India.
